LongMemEval is the benchmark the memory field actually reports against
(Hindsight's 91.4% claim is a LongMemEval number), which makes it reference
material even though we do not compete on it.

## How we use it

- **Method, adopted:** V2's core idea — memory quality is an accuracy/latency
  FRONTIER, not a scalar — was reproduced against our own sealed search
  baseline with their unmodified scorer. The Pareto discipline stuck; the
  single LAFS number did not.
- **Score, declined:** our judged retrieval fixture measures search relevance
  over a real docs corpus, not conversational QA; the numbers are not
  comparable, and pretending otherwise would be leaderboard theater.
