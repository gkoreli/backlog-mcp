The embedding model is the one piece of ML in the product, and it runs where
the invariants demand: locally, at install, with no API call. all-MiniLM-L6-v2
maps text to 384-dim vectors; the Xenova repository is the ONNX conversion that
Transformers.js loads, and sentence-transformers/all-MiniLM-L6-v2 is the
upstream fine-tune. The MiniLM paper (Wang et al. 2020) is the distillation
technique the architecture descends from.

## Costs we accept, for now

- ~90 MB fp32 download at install (audit F22).
- Short context window: long documents embed as one truncated vector; the
  deterministic heading-path chunking contingency (0116 R-8) is the answer if
  the tail gate fails.

Succession is a catalogued program, not a drift: candidates and their evidence
live in the idea garden, and no swap ships without beating the sealed baseline
on the judged fixture.
