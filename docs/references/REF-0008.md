Loro is the one stack candidate captured here in the PARKED state, and the
parking reason is itself a load-bearing decision: the product's truth is
committed frontmatter markdown, and no history substrate may demote it.

## What would change if it were picked

- Every memory correction, supersede chain, and status transition becomes
  browsable history with time travel — "one source of truth per fact" gains a
  time axis.
- The pick is only valid in the derived position: markdown authoritative, Loro
  a rebuildable projection (or global-home-only). ADR 0107 as written (Loro as
  sole truth) is explicitly not adopted.
