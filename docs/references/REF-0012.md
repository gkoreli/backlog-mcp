Hindsight is the strongest recent claims-space entrant: a memory architecture
that takes epistemology seriously (what KIND of claim is this memory?) and
posted the first 90%+ LongMemEval result.

## What survived our reproduction

The reproduction annotated 48 minimal propositions from live memories and
ADR claims against a frozen rubric. The typology is real — facts, beliefs,
and experiences distribute differently and decay differently — but the
record-level enum did not survive contact: too many real memories mix types
in one claim unit. So we keep the hierarchy as design guidance (beliefs are
contradiction-revisable; facts supersede) and decline the schema field.

This is the reference pattern working as intended: an external idea, a frozen
rubric, a reproduction on our corpus, and a narrowed adoption.
